Anonymous: Where and how did you learn how to make digital art?

I learned from a multitude of places, but mainly from several friends over at a chatroom.

Things I can advise for you to learn is to check /mlp/’s drawfag improvement thread , (the imgur albums will be particularly useful) and to seek feedback from them in a regular basis- they are good folk and more than willing to throw you a hand. This chatroom at Discord might also be useful, but I don’t know how active it is. Running a quest in /mlp/ might also be a good way to learn, since it’ll provide regular practice.

And remember: The core of learning anything is practice, so if you want to learn how to draw, draw.
