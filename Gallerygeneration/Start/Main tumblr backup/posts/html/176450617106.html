Tritons are a race from Cirquesque capable of surviving in land or water, although staying for too long in one exclusively makes their cartilaginous bones either too hard or too soft.
They are known for their sexual depravity, total disregard of personal space, and liking towards body modding. It’s rare to find a ‘clean’ one as the ones depicted here, as most will have piercings , tattoos, skin pockets, you mention it.
Sirens regard them as their bastard cousins, and most other people doesn’t like them much either, but they are valued guards due to their durability -they are to squishy to harm by blunt force- and adherence to oaths.
